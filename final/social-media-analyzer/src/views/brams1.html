Real-Time Example

How BRAMS Protects a New Phone Launch

See how BRAMS monitors, analyzes of a newly launched phone in real-time.

1

Real-Time Monitoring

As soon as the phone is launched, BRAMS starts monitoring social media platforms, news websites, and review sites for any mentions of the phone. It tracks hashtags, keywords, and even images related to the product.

2

Sentiment Analysis

BRAMS analyzes the sentiment of each mention, identifying positive, negative, and neutral comments. It detects subtle emotions like frustration or excitement, and even understands sarcasm or cultural nuances in different languages.

3

Crisis Detection

If a negative review or complaint starts gaining traction, BRAMS identifies it as a potential crisis. It analyzes patterns from past incidents to predict how the issue might escalate and alerts the PR team immediately.

4

Autonomous Response

For common issues like delivery delays or minor complaints, BRAMS generates an appropriate response in the brand's voice. For more serious issues, it escalates the matter to the human team.

5

Continuous Improvement

BRAMS learns from each interaction, improving its response strategies over time. It tracks the effectiveness of its responses and adjusts its approach to better protect the brand's reputation.

What platforms does BRAMS support?

BRAMS supports all major social media platforms, including Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, Reddit and YouTube. It also monitors news websites, blogs, review platforms like Trustpilot and Yelp, and online forums.

How does BRAMS handle multiple languages?

BRAMS uses advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) to understand and analyze content in over 50 languages. It can detect cultural nuances, regional variations, and even sarcasm or humor in different languages.

We offer 24/7 customer support via email, chat, and phone. Enterprise clients also have access to a dedicated account manager and priority support.
